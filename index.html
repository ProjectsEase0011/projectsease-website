FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is the support material plagiarism-free?+

Yes. All reference material is prepared with originality in mind, and we provide plagiarism-reduction guidance and proper citation practices to help you maintain academic integrity.

Do you provide SPSS and statistical analysis guidance?+

Absolutely. We guide you through SPSS, regression, OLS, Tobit, IV models and more — explaining how to run tests and interpret outputs so you understand your own analysis.

What is the typical delivery timeline?+

Timelines depend on project complexity and your responsiveness. After consultation, we agree on a clear, realistic schedule that respects your deadlines.

Do you provide revisions?+

Yes. Reasonable revisions within the agreed project scope are included. Additional revisions beyond scope may involve extra charges.

Is my information and payment secure?+

Complete confidentiality is guaranteed — your files, data and identity are never shared with third parties. Payments are handled through secure, mutually agreed channels.

Which subjects and levels do you support?+

We support college, university, MBA, PhD, nursing, engineering and research scholars across 15+ disciplines including management, healthcare, VLSI, economics and more.

⚖️ Service Disclaimer

Projects Ease provides academic guidance, research support, consultation, formatting assistance, statistical analysis guidance, and educational support services strictly for learning, reference, mentoring, and research assistance purposes only.

All materials, drafts, reports, analyses, presentations, and documents provided are intended to serve as reference and support material to help students understand research methodologies, academic structures, formatting standards, and analytical approaches. Students and researchers are solely responsible for reviewing, modifying, and using the provided support material in accordance with their university, college, institutional, and academic policies.

Projects Ease does not claim ownership of institutional submissions made by users and does not guarantee grades, approvals, certifications, publication acceptance, or academic outcomes.

Policies & Terms

Transparent & fair, always.

Projects Ease believes in complete transparency and professional commitment. Our policies ensure fairness, quality service, and smooth collaboration for both parties.

Payment Policy+
  • Clients must pay a 30% advance payment before the project begins.
  • Remaining payment must be completed before final delivery of editable/source files.
  • Work will only begin after confirmation of advance payment.
Refund Policy+
  • Advance payments are strictly non-refundable once the project has started.
  • As services involve consultation, research support, analysis, formatting, designing, mentoring and time investment, refunds are not applicable after work initiation.
  • If the client cancels midway, the advance payment will not be refunded.
  • No refunds for completed or partially completed work.
  • Delays caused by incomplete information or delayed client responses do not qualify for refunds.
Revision Policy+
  • Reasonable revisions are provided within the agreed project scope.
  • Additional revisions beyond scope may involve extra charges.
Confidentiality Policy+
  • All client information, files, research data and communication remain confidential.
  • No client information will be shared with third parties.
Delivery Policy+
  • Delivery timelines depend on project complexity and client responsiveness.
  • Delays caused by incomplete information or late responses may affect timelines.

Clients are expected to use all provided materials ethically and responsibly. Services are intended to assist students in learning, research understanding, data interpretation, formatting, presentation preparation and academic development.
